About Audio Studio Pro

Welcome to Audio Studio Pro, your complete digital studio for Indian classical music and professional audio editing. Our platform is built by musicians, for musicians, bridging the gap between time-honored practice traditions and modern digital utility.

The Foundation: The Digital Tanpura

Tanpura is the unwavering soul of Indian classical music, providing the essential sonic anchor, the Shruti. Our Digital Tanpura is meticulously engineered to replicate the rich, complex resonance of a hand-tuned acoustic instrument.

Authentic Practice Partner

  • Authentic Sound sampled from high-quality acoustic instruments.
  • Precision Tuning for perfect pitch in any Raga or scale.
  • Consistent, unwavering drone available 24/7.

The Suite of Audio Tools

Beyond practice, Audio Studio Pro is a robust audio toolkit designed to help you analyze, modify, and master your recordings.

Pitch Analyzer

Determine the precise note/pitch range within any uploaded audio.

Recorder

Captures live audio directly in the browser.

Pitcher

Adjusts the key of an audio file without altering its tempo.

Splitter

Splits a single audio file into multiple, manageable segments.

Key & BPM Finder

Automatically detects the musical key and Beats Per Minute.

Cutter

Precisely trim and remove unwanted parts from audio tracks.

Joiner

Combines several separate audio files into one cohesive track.

Remover

Isolates or eliminates elements like vocals or instrumental tracks.
